Investigation Timeline

Chronological overview of key events in the investigation.

2005-2006

Initial Investigation

Palm Beach Police Department begins investigation following complaints from parents of minor victims.

2007

FBI Involvement

FBI opens investigation. Search warrants executed at Palm Beach property.

June 2008

Non-Prosecution Agreement

DOJ enters controversial NPA with Epstein. Later ruled to have violated the Crime Victims' Rights Act.

July 6, 2019

Arrest at Teterboro Airport

Arrested by FBI-NYPD Crimes Against Children Task Force upon arrival from Paris.

July 8, 2019

Federal Indictment

Indicted on sex trafficking charges by SDNY. Full evidence list created.

August 10, 2019

Death in Custody

Found deceased at Metropolitan Correctional Center. Ruled suicide.

July 2, 2020

Maxwell Arrest

Ghislaine Maxwell arrested in New Hampshire by FBI.

December 29, 2021

Maxwell Conviction

Found guilty on 5 of 6 counts including sex trafficking of a minor.

June 28, 2022

Maxwell Sentencing

Sentenced to 20 years in federal prison.

January 2024

Document Releases Begin

Court-ordered unsealing of documents from Giuffre v. Maxwell civil case.

December 2025
